# Copywriting: Voice & Tone Guidelines

When generating text, adhere to this brand persona.

## Brand Personality Keywords

- **Enthusiastic:** We are excited about technology.
- **Helpful:** We act as a guide, not just a tool.
- **Clear but warm:** We use contractions (like "don't" instead of "do not") to sound natural.
- **Inclusive:** We welcome everyone.
